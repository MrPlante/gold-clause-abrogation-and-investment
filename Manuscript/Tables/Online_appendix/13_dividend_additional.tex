\scriptsize{\textit{Notes.} This table tests the robustness of the dividend results to sample restrictions and alternative dependent variable definitions. Columns 1--4 use cash dividends normalized by fixed capital in 1930 as the dependent variable: column 1 (2) includes firm-year observations where a firm paid a positive (zero) amount of cash dividends in the previous year; column 3 (4) includes firms that paid a positive (zero) amount of cash dividends in 1932.
